Paul Darey was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina in 1977 and moved to Barcelona in 1988. In the early nineties he became influenced by electronic music, and started playing nightly in a small club in Barcelona called Jerba. By the late nineties he had been massively influenced by the House music that was hitting the Spanish club scene at that time and became a regular in many of Barcelona’s top clubs.

Thanks to his versatile style mixing House, Electro and Progressive house... it allowed him to move over to New York where he stayed for 3 years playing in venues such as Roxy and Splash amongst others. After New York he returned to Barcelona, with parties in Club Catwalk and Discoteq...then on to Ibiza.

Currently we find Paul hitting the clubbing capital of the world, Ibiza. Café Mambo, on the sunset strip of San Antonio, was Paul’s first residency...a place frequented by some of the islands most beautiful clubbers and top music industry people meeting there throughout the summer months. Paul found him self playing alongside djs such as Roger Sanchez for his pre-party Release Yourself and for events organised by radio stations like BBC Radio 1 & Galaxy Radio. He also joined the Cafe Mambo World Tour team djing all around the world. After Cafe Mambo Paul moved his residency to one of El Divino'd most important parties Salvacion Ibiza...playing weekly for one of the most glamours nights of the week.

In 2008 Paul went onto securing a residency in Ibiza's most famous of super clubs....SPACE! Starting with Mondays Calling playing on the main terrace, for dates at We Love Sundays and then for their seasonal closing parties...perhaps the most important closing party in the world!! 2010 saw Paul return to Space stronger than ever with his new residency with Salvacion Ibiza's Republik which had moved from El Divino to Space every Saturday on the main terrace in collaboration with Cafe Ole. He has build himself into one of Space's most versatile and hard working residents playing at all the clubs most prestigious events - openings, closings, anniversaries and of course his weekly residency at Republik.

Paul has also joined the Space On Tour's and Republik World Tour's team of Djs touring extensively throughout the world. Over the last few years Paul has fond him self playing in venues such as; The EGG London, Scala London, THAT London.
